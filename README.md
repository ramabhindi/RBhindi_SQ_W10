# Week 9 Example 3 — Higher or Lower

READ ME SECTION

Play higher or lower, choose higher or lower from a deck of cards. Every correct answer gets you closer to the next level. Don't choose incorrectly, as you will lose and have to restart.

This game has been tweaked from Week 9's sidequest to incorporate 3 changes. Below are the 3 changes that I've incorporated:

Change 1: Make "equal" cards a correct play. In the original game, equal cards would count as a loss. Now, if you choose higher/lower but pull the same card, it will count as a win. The odds are slim, and i feel like players should be awarded for the luck rather than penalized.

Change 2: JOKER cards. To compensate for making the game easier by making equal plays wins, there is now an equal chance that a JOKER card will show up. The joker card doesn't end your run, but sets you back 1 point. (technically 2, but the guaranteed right answer gives a point back) JOKERS can't be the very first card. The card value of a joker is 0, meaning the only correct answer is to go higher. If you choose lower on a joker, you lose.

Change 3: WOMP WOMP sound when the player loses. No reference was made for this SFX, because it is literally my voice saying "womp womp"

Debugging menu - Press D to open the debugging menu, with warp points to go to different levels, or even the win, intro, and game over screens.

Press D again to close the debugging menu.
